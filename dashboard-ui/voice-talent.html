Who owns my voice?

You do. Always. We train a model from your sample, but you approve every use case before it goes live. You can revoke access at any time and we will destroy the model immediately.

Where does my data go?

Nowhere. Everything runs on bare metal in Canada. Zero cloud. Zero third-party APIs. Your voice sample and trained model never leave our physical servers.

How do I get paid?

50/50 revenue share paid monthly. We support direct deposit, PayPal, and Bitcoin (BTCPay). You see exactly what your voice earned in your dashboard.

Can I mail a physical recording?

Yes. Record on any device, save to an SD card or USB drive, and mail it to us. We process the audio, train the model, and mail your card back or destroy it — your choice. Contact us for the mailing address.

What languages do you need most?

Every language. But right now we're especially looking for Chinese (Mandarin & Cantonese), Japanese, Korean, Hindi, Arabic, and Russian voices. Be the first voice for your country.

Do I need professional equipment?

A quiet room and a decent microphone is enough to start. We'll work with you on quality. If you're a professional voice actor with studio recordings, even better.

What will my voice be used for?

Audiobook narration, tutorial videos, AI assistant responses, game characters, and more — always with your approval. You choose which projects your voice appears in.
